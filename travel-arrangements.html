Travel Arrangements

Location

Palm Springs Resort is located in the Eastern region of Africa in the country of Wakanda and the town of Omunye

From the Airport

For those who so wish the hotel has a chauffeur service with highly credit drivers.

This means that transport will be provided from the hotel upon request.

By road

The hotel can be accessed by road

Once you cross the Bikito bridge follow St. Kimani road for approximately five (5) Kilometeres before turning right onto Mtumbba Road.

The hotel is located eight hundred (800) Meters ahead.
